Fuel Efficiency Report
Actual vs ideal consumption — cost of inefficiency per vehicle
How the loss is calculated: For each vehicle we compare actual litres used vs ideal litres for the same distance. The extra litres × average price per litre = KSh lost.
Extra litres = (KMs ÷ Actual KM/L) − (KMs ÷ Ideal KM/L)  →  × Avg Price/L = KSh Lost
